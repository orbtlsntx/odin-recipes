Dad's Brilliant Banana Bread

Bananas, the fruit of all fruits. a healthy pre or post workout snack on it's own these golden phalic wonders are a healthy choice in any diet. That is, until dad gets hold of them. with a few ingredients (and puns thrown in for good measure) he turns these golden potassium laden wonder fruits from a preworkout snack, to a 'reason why you have to workout' snack. I always think to myself; "If there's banana in the title can it really be that bad?" (banana hammock not withstanding) May the trickery begin.

Ingredients
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil>
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4-1/2 cup crushed walnuts
Directions
  1. preheat oven to 325f
  2. combine oil, eggs, sugar, milk vanilla, and mashed bananas
  3. add baking soda and flour
  4. combine well for 2-3 minutes
  5. pour into greased loaf pan and bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes
