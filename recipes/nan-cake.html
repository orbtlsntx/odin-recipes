Ingredients
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350f
  2. In large bowl, stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda , and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. mix for 2 minutes on medium speed mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin, pour evenly into greased and floured pans.
  3. Bake 30-35 minutes in oven until cake is baked in center (use toothpick to check)
  4. Alow to cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan. move to wire rack and allow to cool completely
  5. When cool cover with icing and decorations
